NAIVASHA,THE PLACE TO BE

I had been feeling unmotivated and slumped for a while.It had taken such a huge toll on me to a point where i was struggling to do the bare minimum.This went on for quite sometime until one Friday evening, my husband,Hosek, suggested that we visit Naivasha for the weekend.

Thats how we ended up in my version of heaven on earth.

The serenity and clear air that Naivasha holds is quite frankly to die for. The beautiful landscapes, the exquisite,top-notch hotels, the kind people, the often calm weather...Naivasha spoke to my heart.

We visited a number of places within the town and every stop was another reason to truly appreciate the town.

Here are a few places we visited.

  1. Hell's gate
  2. Lake Naivasha
  3. Mount Longonot

These are just but some of the beautiful places Naivasha has to offer. I left the town feeling relaxed,energized and more of myself.
